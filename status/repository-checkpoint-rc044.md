## The Premise/Implementation Distinction

The central finding of Cycle 7 is a boundary within DAM's transformation capacity:

**What DAM appears to transform:**
- Theoretical (existence) Watch B → suppressed by institutional encoding of causal presence
- Theoretical (optimum) Watch B rooted in implementation disputes → transformed into Methodological Watch B

**What DAM appears unable to transform:**
- Theoretical (optimum) Watch B rooted in premise disputes → formalization of one position cannot resolve a dispute about the biological premises generating the optimum

RC-043 first identified the boundary; RC-044 confirmed it survived explicit DAM engagement. The distinction between premise and implementation disputes is now the operative scope boundary for CMech-001.

## What Cycle 7 Did Not Accomplish

**Wound 6 was not triggered.** RC-043 was the test; the partial result was more nuanced than a clean wound. The revised wound conditions (Wound 6 = premise-level Theoretical Watch B under High DAM; Wound 7 = DAM suppresses a premise-level dispute) are now more precise than the original specification.

**The Watch B type taxonomy is not promoted.** Four Watch B types (Theoretical-existence, Directional, Methodological, Theoretical-optimum) are now candidate distinctions with one to three observations each. Three-instance minimum for promotion applies. None of the four has reached that threshold.

**The premise/implementation distinction is not established.** Two observations support it. Anti-compression applies. It is the operating scope refinement candidate for CMech-001, not a confirmed property of the mechanism.

---

## What Cycle 7 Did Accomplish

**The mechanism candidate is narrower.** CMech-001 before Cycle 7 claimed DAM transforms Watch B. CMech-001 after Cycle 7 claims DAM transforms specific Watch B sub-types by a specific process (institutional encoding for existence, methodology-implementation resolution for optimum-implementation). The narrower claim is more informative and more falsifiable.

**The scope boundary survived pressure.** RC-044 was designed to attack the scope boundary specifically. The boundary held. Two observations supporting a scope boundary that survived a direct test is a qualitatively stronger position than two observations that simply confirmed the pattern.

**The mechanism phase is more specific.** Scope delimitation is now asking a specific question — can premise-level disputes be transformed by DAM? — rather than the general question of mechanism existence. More specific phases generate more informative audits.

## Orientation for Next Cycle

The three highest-value test types for the next phase:

**Test type 1 — Wound 7 opportunity:** A High DAM domain where an optimum-level premise dispute was resolved by subsequent evidence (RCT, mechanistic study) rather than by methodological capitulation. This would test whether premise disputes can be settled from outside the DAM framework, with DAM then formalizing the result.

**Test type 2 — Completed trajectory:** A High DAM domain where both existence and optimum disputes are settled, showing pure APG with minimal Watch B of any type. This would be the endpoint of the trajectory and would demonstrate that the Theoretical (optimum) sub-type is not permanent.

**Test type 3 — Low DAM + contested optimum:** A domain with Low DAM and active optimum-level debate, to determine whether Theoretical (optimum) Watch B requires existence-level resolution as a precondition or can appear without it.

The selection of RC-045 should target one of these three test types rather than a fourth confirmation of the RC-043/RC-044 pair structure.
