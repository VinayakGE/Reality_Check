**Level 2 — Watch B Transformation Hypothesis / CMech-001 Prediction 2:**

If Watch B is methodological (confounding concern with no competing causal mechanism), CMech-001 Prediction 2 is challenged: NEA alone was sufficient to transform Watch B type without DAM.
