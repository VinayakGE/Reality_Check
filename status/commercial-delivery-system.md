# Commercial Delivery System — Decision Review Cycle

**Status:** Working commercial specification  
**Date:** 2026-06-25  
**Product category (external):** Decision Review Cycle  
**Core capability:** Decision Drift Detection  
**Methodology placement:** Reality Check as internal engine, not external category language.

---

## Positioning

The commercial wedge is decision governance over time, not research-quality tooling.

Primary customer promise:

> We protect important strategic decisions from silent drift by tracking assumptions, evidence changes, and review triggers.

---

## 6-Week Pilot Cadence (Fixed)

| Week | Outcome |
|---|---|
| 1 | Map 10–25 active strategic decisions and explicit supporting assumptions |
| 2 | Build a living decision ledger with evidence links and competing explanations |
| 3 | Detect decision drift: weakened/falsified assumptions, stale rationale, and trigger conditions |
| 4 | Run structured stakeholder review sessions and update decision records |
| 5 | Produce prioritized actions: Maintain / Revisit / Defer / Retire |
| 6 | Deliver board-ready outputs and launch ongoing review cadence |

Pilot promise:

> By the end of six weeks, you will know which strategic decisions are healthy, which have silently drifted, and which deserve immediate review.

---

## Mandatory Executive Artifacts (Pilot Exit Criteria)

### 1) Decision Health Dashboard
- Active decisions
- Health state: Healthy / Monitor / Review / Critical
- Last evidence update
- Next scheduled review

### 2) Decision Drift Report
- Assumptions that changed
- Competing explanations that strengthened
- Decisions requiring immediate attention

### 3) Decision Review Memo
- Decision under review
- Original rationale
- Evidence since decision
- Assumptions that held
- Assumptions that weakened
- Recommendation
- Confidence level
- Next review trigger
