## What RC-020 Should Do

The checkpoint identifies one specific test worth running at RC-020:

> Does a claim with strong source-context causal identification and a cross-context inference structure suppress Watch B — or does Watch B fire anyway?

A cross-context inference claim where the source-context evidence is unusually strong (multiple independent datasets, quasi-experimental variation, long time series) would be the highest-information test. If Watch B is absent in a cross-context inference claim with strong identification, the candidate explanation strengthens. If Watch B fires despite strong identification, the candidate explanation is pressured.
