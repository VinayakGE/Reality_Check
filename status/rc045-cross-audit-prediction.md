## Stage 5 Entry Audit — Why RC-045 Is Different

For RC-038 through RC-044, the maturity gradient and CMech-001 made identical predictions for the same reason. RC-045 breaks the correlation. Returns to education has:
- High evidential capacity (strong NEA, well-developed causal methodology, extensive replication)

CMech-001 and Alternative A now predict different outcomes. The selection of this domain is constrained by the Stage 5 entry conditions committed before this domain was chosen (see `status/rsq-002-maturity-gradient-mechanism.md`, Stage 5 Entry Conditions section).

## Step 2: Institutional Authority Assessment

This assessment is frozen before claim evidence is reviewed. It is the critical variable for Alternative A (Prestige/Gatekeeping).

**Prediction: Returns to education lacks a centralized attribution authority comparable to WHO, GBD, IARC, or Cochrane.**

Evidence for this prediction:
- No institution analogous to IARC has classified "education causes earnings increases" through a formal Group 1 / Group 2 / Group 3 process
- No institution analogous to GBD produces an annual "education-attributable earnings burden" estimate with a named comparative risk methodology applied to population distributions
- The Mincer framework is distributed across the labor economics profession; no single institution holds gatekeeping authority over its application
- The World Bank (Psacharopoulos and Patrinos) produces global returns-to-education reviews, but these are policy research, not causal certification — their estimates are reported alongside alternative estimates without a veto mechanism
- Top journals (American Economic Review, Journal of Labor Economics, Quarterly Journal of Economics) publish both affirming and skeptical returns-to-education papers without a gatekeeping authority determining which methodological tradition is authoritative

**Caveat:** The economics profession does have prestige hierarchies. Top-journal publication and NBER affiliation function as quality signals. The question for Alternative A is whether these function as *causal-existence gatekeeping* — preventing Watch B claims from being published or taken seriously — or merely as *quality filtering* that does not prevent heterodox causal claims from circulating. The pre-registration prediction is: the economics profession's prestige structures are not strong enough, and not specifically organized around causal-existence determination, to function as effective Watch B suppression in the way IARC or GBD does.

**What each outcome would mean:**

**If Watch B is minimal (APG primary, Watch B absent or faint):**
CMech-001 supported; Alternative A pressured. The suppression of Watch B occurred despite weak centralized institutional authority — consistent with evidential capacity (not gatekeeping) doing the work. The DAM/institutional-authority correlation is broken for the first time.

**If Watch B is materially active (present as Theoretical or Directional, not merely Methodological residual):**
Alternative A supported; CMech-001 needs revision. Strong evidential capacity without institutional gatekeeping was insufficient to suppress Watch B. Something about the centralized certification process in the Cycle 7 domains was necessary, not merely downstream of evidential capacity.

**If Watch B is present but only Methodological (no competing theoretical claims, only confounding concerns):**
Most informative intermediate result: CMech-001's claim that DAM transforms Watch B type may hold even without institutional authority. Watch B transformed from Theoretical to Methodological through methodological capacity (IV literature) alone — without IARC-style certification. This would refine both CMech-001 (institutional authority not necessary for transformation, only for suppression) and Alternative A (institutional authority affects Watch B strength but not type).

## The Specific Claim

**Predicted audit claim:** A version of the Mincer/Becker/Psacharopoulos-Patrinos consensus: "Schooling has substantial causal effects on individual earnings — each additional year of education increases wages by approximately 8–10%, a finding replicated across contexts and identification strategies, justifying public investment in education as an economic development priority."

**Predicted primary explanatory alternatives:**
- H1: Signaling (Spence 1973, Arrow 1973) — education does not build human capital; it signals pre-existing ability. Returns to education are a selection artifact, not a causal effect.
- H2: Heterogeneous returns / LATE — IV estimates identify local average treatment effects for marginal students; population-average causal effects may differ substantially.
- H3: Institutional credential effects — returns reflect occupational licensing, credential requirements, and degree inflation rather than human capital or signaling.

**Predicted Watch B character (to be confirmed or disconfirmed by audit):** Minimal — the signaling debate is heterodox but not a live existence-level dispute in the sense of Theoretical (existence) Watch B. The mainstream research program accepts that education has some human capital effect; the debate is about mechanism and magnitude shares. This prediction follows from CMech-001 (strong NEA + DAM-equivalent methodology suppressed existence-level Watch B) rather than Alternative A.

## What Would Constitute Alternative A Support

Alternative A would be supported if the audit finds:

1. An active Theoretical (existence) Watch B — a research program that genuinely contests whether education causes earnings increases, distinct from the heterodox signaling literature (which is present but does not dominate mainstream claims or agenda-setting)
2. The active Watch B is plausibly sustained by the absence of institutional authority — researchers maintain the skeptical position specifically because there is no IARC/WHO-equivalent to challenge their publication prospects
3. Watch B's strength is comparable to or stronger than Methodological Watch B in RC-041 (Air Pollution), where institutional authority was high

Alternative A would be pressured if Watch B is minimal despite weak centralized institutional authority, and the remaining frontier disputes are APG-structured (contribution shares, magnitude, mechanism pathway between education and earnings).

## Predicted Non-Appearance

**Counterfactual Unavailability:** Absent. The natural experiment literature explicitly constructs counterfactuals through IV strategies and randomized program evaluations. Psacharopoulos and Patrinos explicitly estimate returns relative to alternative uses of time.

**Implementation-Documentation Limitation:** Unlikely to dominate. The research program has extensive documentation of its methodology and findings.
