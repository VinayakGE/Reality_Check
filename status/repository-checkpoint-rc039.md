## 4. DEVTA Effect-Modification Observation — Candidate Watch Item

RC-039 flagged an edge case: VAS reduces child mortality conditionally on vitamin A deficiency prevalence, and the dispute between the Cochrane 24% estimate and DEVTA's 4% finding involves the question of whether contemporary populations are sufficiently deficient to realize the documented effect.

This is within APG's scope — it is a contribution-share question (how much of the effect is realized in current programmatic populations?) — but it has a structure that could eventually exceed APG if the condition itself becomes the frontier rather than the magnitude.

**Current status:** One instance. Not a new object. A candidate watch item.

**Activation condition for further attention:** A second audit where an accepted cause's effect is disputed not because its magnitude is uncertain, but because the precondition for its operation is unknown in the target population. If this structure recurs, an APG sub-category for conditional-effect disputes may be warranted.

## 8. RC-040 Candidate Selection

Candidates scored at Intermediate in the operationalization document:
- **Candidate A — Minimum Wage Effects on Employment** (1 High, 3 Intermediate)
- **Candidate C — Microfinance and Poverty Reduction** (2 Intermediate, 1 Low, 1 High — effectively Intermediate)

Both are Intermediate-maturity. The choice between them is substantive rather than structural. Minimum Wage has a larger natural experiment literature (Card-Krueger, state-border designs) and is more actively contested between research programs with clear institutional identities (Dube et al. vs. Neumark et al.). That active between-program controversy is exactly where APG structure tends to appear at intermediate maturity. Microfinance has more RCTs but smaller effect sizes and the earlier observational literature has largely been superseded.

Minimum Wage is the marginally stronger diagnostic test because the research programs have clearer institutional identity and the dispute's structure is better documented.
