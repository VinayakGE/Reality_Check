# RC-046 Cross-Audit Prediction

**Date recorded:** 2026-06-24
**Status:** Pre-registered — audit not yet conducted

This prediction is the twelfth cross-audit ontology prediction and the ninth under independent predictor measurement. Its specific role: first domain where Low Prestige Coordination and non-trivial DAM co-exist — the correlation-breaking design RSQ-002 requires for CMech-001 vs. narrowed Alternative A to generate divergent predictions.

---

## Why RC-046 Is Different From RC-045

RC-045 separated formal certification from prestige coordination. The retroactive prestige-coordination scoring (committed in `status/prestige-coordination-operationalization.md`) revealed that returns to education has approximately High-Intermediate prestige coordination — the Angrist/Card/Duflo citation cluster, AER/QJE journal dominance, and NBER concentration give economics a coordinated prestige structure even without formal certification.

RC-045 therefore tested: High PC without formal certification. CMech-001 and narrowed Alternative A made similar predictions for that domain because economics has substantial prestige coordination.

RC-046 tests: Low PC with non-trivial DAM. No prior repository domain has this profile. CMech-001 and narrowed Alternative A now make genuinely different predictions.

## Step 3: Divergent Predictions — Watch B Type as Discriminant

The discriminant output is not Watch B presence or absence. It is Watch B *type*. The repository's most diagnostic mechanism-competition evidence has come from type distinctions rather than presence/absence distinctions (RC-041 established Watch B type as more informative than Watch B strength; RC-042 through RC-044 confirmed this).

### Claim to be audited

"Psychotherapy effectively treats mental health conditions — effect sizes are clinically meaningful and the evidence base is robust enough to support psychotherapy as a first-line treatment for depression, anxiety disorders, and other common mental health conditions."

**Sources:** Cuijpers P et al. (multiple meta-analyses including Cuijpers et al. 2019, *World Psychiatry*); Wampold BE and Imel ZE (2015), *The Great Psychotherapy Debate*; Lambert MJ (2013), *Bergin and Garfield's Handbook of Psychotherapy and Behavior Change*; APA Division 12 empirically supported treatments lists; NICE guidelines (UK); Hofmann SG et al. (CBT-specific systematic reviews).

### Mechanisms generating divergent predictions

**CMech-001 (DAM drives Watch B transformation):**

Intermediate DAM — named manuals, adherence measures, RCT methodology as named standard, network meta-analysis — should be sufficient to suppress existence-level Theoretical Watch B even under Low Prestige Coordination. If DAM is doing the work, the research community's fragmentation does not prevent the causal-identification tools from establishing general efficacy. The frontier of dispute should have moved to:

- APG: Which modality contributes how much? Common factors vs. specific techniques contribution share?
- Methodological: Researcher allegiance bias; efficacy vs. effectiveness gap; RCT internal validity vs. external validity?
- Modality-specific heterogeneity: Does CBT outperform psychodynamic? By how much? For whom?

**CMech-001 predicted Watch B character:** Methodological and/or Directional (modality superiority), not Theoretical (existence-level general efficacy). The question "does psychotherapy work at all?" should not be a live research-program frontier.

---

**Narrowed Alternative A (Prestige Coordination drives Watch B suppression):**

Low Prestige Coordination means no network of journals, citation clusters, and review bodies converging on the causal claim. Fragmented camps — CBT, psychodynamic, common factors, humanistic, third-wave — each maintain their own legitimacy structures without coordinating around a shared conclusion. Under this account, the existence-level dispute should remain active because no coordination mechanism marginalizes the skeptical positions:

- The "does psychotherapy outperform active placebo / non-specific supportive contact?" question should be live
- The common factors debate (is there any specific efficacy beyond relationship factors?) should constitute Theoretical Watch B
- The Dodo Bird Verdict vs. CBT-specificity debate should not have converged despite hundreds of RCTs

**Narrowed Alternative A predicted Watch B character:** Theoretical (existence-level or directional at the general efficacy level) — specifically the question of whether there is any specific efficacy beyond non-specific supportive contact should be an active existence-level dispute, not merely an APG-structured contribution-share question.

### The Decisive Observation

| Watch B outcome | Implication |
|---|---|
| Watch B Methodological or absent at existence level | CMech-001 cleared a harder test than RC-045; DAM sufficient without PC; Alternative A's prestige-coordination version directly pressured |
| Watch B Theoretical (existence: "does therapy work at all?") materially active | Alternative A gains first direct supporting observation; prestige coordination needed for Watch B suppression; CMech-001 revision required |
| Watch B Directional at modality level (CBT vs. psychodynamic) but not existence level | Intermediate result: existence-level Watch B absent (CMech-001 partial support), but modality-level Watch B active (consistent with low PC affecting modality disputes even if not existence disputes) |

**The third outcome would be the most novel finding:** It would suggest CMech-001 and Alternative A both operate, but at different levels of the causal claim — DAM suppresses existence-level Watch B; PC affects whether modality-superiority disputes escalate to active Watch B or remain APG-structured. That would require further refinement of both mechanisms.

## Predicted Non-Appearance

**Counterfactual Unavailability:** Likely absent for general efficacy claims; RCTs with waitlist controls construct explicit counterfactuals. May be partially present for long-run outcome questions or for claims about specific real-world effectiveness.

**Maturity Gradient Alignment:** Intermediate maturity → APG primary, Watch B secondary. This is the gradient's standard prediction for Intermediate domains. The mechanism competition test is whether Watch B's character is Methodological (CMech-001) or Theoretical (narrowed Alternative A) — both are consistent with Watch B secondary in the gradient sense, but they diverge at the type level.

|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| NEA | Intermediate | High | Intermediate | High | Intermediate (lean Low) |
| DAM | High | Low | High | Intermediate-High | Intermediate |

| Watch B | Methodological | Theoretical | Theoretical+Methodological | Methodological | Unknown |

RC-046 is the first domain in the repository where Prestige Coordination is cleanly Low while DAM remains at Intermediate. If CMech-001's mechanism (DAM does the work) is correct, Watch B should behave as it did in RC-041 (Methodological or weaker) — despite Low PC. If narrowed Alternative A's mechanism (PC does the work) is correct, Watch B should behave as it did in RC-042 or RC-035 (Theoretical) — despite Intermediate DAM.

That is the test. The audit result has a clean failure mode for both mechanisms.
