# Decision Review Cycle Pilot (6 Weeks)

## Offer Summary

The Decision Review Cycle is a structured operating cadence that helps organizations identify decision drift and refresh institutional reasoning on active strategic decisions.

---

## Scope

- 10–25 active strategic decisions
- Assumption mapping for each decision
- Evidence and competing explanation updates
- Leadership review facilitation
- Board-ready outputs

---

## 6-Week Cadence

| Week | Outcome |
|---|---|
| 1 | Decision inventory + assumption mapping |
| 2 | Build living decision ledger |
| 3 | Drift detection + trigger mapping |
| 4 | Structured stakeholder review sessions |
| 5 | Prioritized recommendations (Maintain / Revisit / Defer / Retire) |
| 6 | Board-ready delivery + recurring cadence launch |

---

## Deliverables

1. Decision Health Dashboard  
2. Decision Drift Report  
3. Decision Review Memos for priority decisions  
4. Recurring governance cadence (roles, frequency, triggers)

---

## Commercial Terms

- **Pilot price:** $10k–15k (fixed-fee, 6 weeks)
- **Post-pilot program:** $3k–8k/month for smaller organizations (scope-based)
- **Enterprise / multi-team:** custom

---

## Pilot Promise

By the end of six weeks, your team will know which strategic decisions are healthy, which have drifted, and which deserve immediate review.
