## Problem

Most organizations retain decisions, but lose decision reasoning:
- Why the decision was made
- Which alternatives were rejected
- What evidence would change the decision
- When the decision should be reviewed

Within 6–18 months, strategic memory decays and drift goes unnoticed.

---

## Solution

### Decision Ledger (Platform)
Living record of strategic decisions, assumptions, evidence links, competing explanations, confidence, and review triggers.

### Decision Drift Detection (Flagship capability)
Signals when assumptions weaken, alternatives strengthen, and immediate review is warranted.

### Decision Review Cycle (Service)
Structured six-week operating cadence to establish governance and board-ready outputs.
