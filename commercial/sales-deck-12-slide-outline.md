# Sales Deck Outline (12 Slides)

## 1) Title
- Reality Check
- Decision Ledger + Decision Drift Detection
- Protect strategic decisions from silent drift

## 2) The Executive Problem
- Organizations retain decisions but lose decision reasoning
- Institutional memory decays in 6–18 months

## 3) Cost of Silent Drift
- Stale assumptions
- Missed trigger events
- Repeated strategic mistakes

## 4) Product Architecture
- Reality Check (company/methodology)
- Decision Review Cycle (service)
- Decision Ledger (platform)
- Decision Drift Detection (core capability)

## 5) How Decision Drift Detection Works
- Monitor assumption status
- Detect stronger competing explanations
- Trigger priority review

## 6) 6-Week Pilot Cadence
- Week-by-week outcomes and governance setup

## 7) Executive Outputs
- Decision Health Dashboard
- Decision Drift Report
- Decision Review Memo

## 8) Example Executive Readout
- Decision: Expand Program X
- Assumptions weakened/falsified
- Recommendation + trigger

## 9) Ideal Customer Profile
- Tier 1: Foundations, grant-making orgs, policy think tanks
- Tier 2: VC/family office/corporate strategy

## 10) Commercial Model
- Pilot: $10k–15k
- Recurring: $3k–8k/month
- Enterprise: custom

## 11) Delivery Progression
- Service first
- Software-assisted workflow second
- SaaS third

## 12) Close / CTA
- Book Decision Review Cycle pilot
- Define first 10–25 strategic decisions
